About this project

I will say, developing and executing this project was more daunting than I initially thought it would be. Nonetheless, this was still a lot of fun to make. For myself, I am a senior at Oakland University and a major in Computer Science. I have been mesmorized by technology since a young age; the possibilities are truly endless and I learn new things every day.

As I touched on earlier, I am an avid user of Google Calendar. I have had a Google phone for around the last 6 years, and genuinely use the reminder feature everyday. I tried my hardest to emulate such a feature, but settled for an agenda-esq design. I hope the website, especially the plethora of puppies, is acceptable.
